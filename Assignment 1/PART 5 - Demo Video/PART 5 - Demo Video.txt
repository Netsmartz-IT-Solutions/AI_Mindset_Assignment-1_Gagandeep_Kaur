Demo Script

Hi, this is my demo for the AI-assisted engineering task.

I built a simple Task Management REST API with three main endpoints: create a task, update task status, and fetch tasks by user.

I started by understanding the requirement on my own and reducing it into a small, clear scope. Since the task was more about clarity than completeness, I kept the solution simple and used Node.js, Express, and in-memory storage.

I used AI right after that initial understanding. At that point, I already knew the main APIs I needed, so I used AI to validate the structure, suggest cleaner organization, and point out edge cases I might miss. So AI came in early, but not blindly. I first made sure I had my own baseline understanding.

In the project structure, I separated the code into routes, controllers, services, and validators. Initially, AI suggested a more shortcut-based structure, where too much logic was kept inside the routes. I did not go with that directly. Instead, I introduced a cleaner controller-service split and then used AI to help build within that structure.

AI helped me most in speeding up the first draft and surfacing edge cases. It also improved quality by helping me think through things like status validation and clearer error responses a bit faster.

If I had more time, I would add tests, persistent storage, and centralized error handling.

Overall, AI helped me move faster, but I still took ownership of reviewing, refining, and validating the final solution.

“Basically, I used AI to speed up the draft, but I still made the final engineering decisions myself.”
